Maheshwari Creations

Maheshwari Creations company has been founded by Seema Tiwari in 2012.

The company was founded with the vision to popularize the handloom sarees and make it accessible to the people all around the world. We have via eCommerce reached a lot of people.

We have around 30 weavers.Every piece of cloth we sell is made exclusively on handloom.A handloom saree can take anywhere between 10 - 15 days to be woven.
