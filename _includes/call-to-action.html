Seema Tiwari

Maheshwari Creations

Maheshwari Creations company has been founded by Seema Tiwari in 2012. She is an expert committee member in PHD Chamber of Commerce.

The company was founded with the vision to popularize the Royal heritage of handloom sarees and make it accessible to the people all around the world. We have around 30 weavers. Every piece of cloth we sell is made exclusively on handloom.

We have via eCommerce reached a lot of people.

Nidhi Jha

Nidhi Jha is the Digital Marketing Executive. She handles all the online and international clients. She also trains the weavers in eCommerce.
